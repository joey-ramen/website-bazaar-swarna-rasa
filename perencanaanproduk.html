Dalam proyek ini, kelompok kami menghadirkan banyak produk yang atraktif dan menangkap mata dengan harga yang terjangkau, terutama bagi para siswi. Perencanaan produk kami lakukan dengan banyak sekali pertimbangan, terutama mengingat target penjualan kami adalah anak-anak seusia kami, yaitu para siswi. Oleh sebab itu, kami berusaha untuk menyeimbangkan jumlah modal yang dibutuhkan untuk produk-produk tersebut, serta menghitung untung yang diperoleh bagi diri kita. Dengan demikian, kami pun berhasil menentukan harga yang cocok untuk semua produk kami.

Beberapa produk kami juga didasari oleh beberapa nilai budaya, seperti scrunchie batik kami yang berasal dari daerah Rembang, Jawa Tengah. Selain itu, kami juga harus menyediakan produk yang merupakan hasil dari bioteknologi konvensional dan produk yang dibuat dari bahan-bahan yang ramah lingkungan. Produk bioteknologi kami merupakan keripik tempe, yang merupakan salah satu bentuk olahan dari tempe. Produk ramah lingkungan kami adalah lotion, yang dibuat dari bahan-bahan yang dapat diurai oleh lingkungan.

1. Sunny Sip

Sunny Sip adalah minuman es teh segar yang kami jual yang sangat cocok diminum saat cuaca panas. Kami membuat produk ini karena es teh adalah minuman favorit banyak orang dan mudah dinikmati oleh semua kalangan. Kami mengemasnya dalam cup transparan dengan tempelan stiker logo bazar kami agar terlihat menarik dan segar. Satu gelas es teh kami jual dengan harga Rp. 5.000,-/gelas.

Produk kami mendukung tema utama dari booth kami, yaitu tema pantai. Tema ini digambarkan melalui stiker-stiker berbentuk bunga-bunga kamboja, dan juga hewan-hewan laut lainnya. Stiker yang kami jual ini cocok untuk mendekorasi atau menghias barang-barangmu agar terlihat lebih menarik. Setiap lembar dari stiker kami diberi harga Rp. 5.000,-/lembar.

Untuk photobooth, kami menggunakan kamera yang dapat mencetak foto tersebut di saat itu. Kami sudah bekerja sama dalam membuat background yang cocok untuk ditangkap kamera, dan yang mengikuti tema utama booth kami juga. Selain itu, kami juga menyediakan aksesoris DIY berbentuk semangka emas dan maskot kami yang terbuat dari kardus. Satu jepretan di photobooth kami diberi harga Rp. 10.000,-.

Sunny Crunch (Tempeh Chips)

Our tempeh chips are named Sunny Crunch. The making of this product takes a long time, around a week until we get the finished product. The reason we chose this product is because we know that it is one of the most popular snacks among kids, teenagers, and adults. It is a snack that people from any age can enjoy. We initially thought of making frozen yogurt, but some considerations were made. We figured that making frozen yogurt was kind of risky and decided to go with a safer option, which is tempeh. From tempeh itself, we wanted to make a traditional popular snack in Indonesia, which is keripik tempe.

Keripik tempe itself is a popular snack among Indonesian people, from kids to adults. Keripik tempe has a salty and savory flavor that is addictive. Another reason we chose this snack is because it is easier to store, and doesn't really need special care like frozen yogurt which can melt if not stored properly. Keripik tempe is usually served at home gatherings, and usually sold by homemade kitchens. However, making the tempeh itself is not easy. On our first try, our group failed because of the errors in the soy bean and yeast ratio. Our tempeh started to smell bad, and gradually became rotten. Because of that, we learned to be more careful when mixing and measuring the soy beans and the yeast.

To ensure the best quality, fresh tempeh is carefully selected before we fry it. The chips are fried in clean oil at the right temperature so that a crispy texture can be perfectly achieved. We also pay attention to the seasoning to create a savory and slightly salty taste, achieving the keripik tempeh’s iconic taste. For our bazaar, Sunny Crunch is designed to be an affordable snack that can be enjoyed anywhere and anytime. Its freshness and crunchiness is maintained through small plastic bags that allow you to carry it easily. With its crunchy texture and traditional flavor, this product proudly represents one of Indonesia’s favorite snacks. A flavorful crunch was had with each bite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg soy bean
  • 5 gr yeast
  • 1 kg tapioka flour
  • 400 ml water
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Garlic
  • Chicken-flavored flavoring
Instructions:
  1. First, prepare all the ingredients.
  2. The soybeans are washed at least three times until they are clean.
  3. Prepare a big pot to brew the soybeans. Pour water into the pot three times the height of the soybeans’ height.
  4. Brew for 45 minutes after the water boils.
  5. After boiling the soybeans, their skin will resurface. Let it be overnight to let more skin resurface.
  6. Peel the skin off the soybeans, and let them dry afterwards.
  7. Yeast is poured into the soybeans, then mixed evenly.
  8. Put 250 grams of soybeans into a plastic bag. Poke holes on both sides of the plastic.
  9. Let the soybeans ferment for 2 days.
  10. After the fermentation process, mix the tempeh with tapioca flour with 1:1 ratio. Mix until the flour coats the entirety of the tempe.
  11. Put the tempeh mixed with tapioca into plastic bags, then shape them into cylinders.
  12. Just like before, poke holes in each side of the cylinder for another fermentation to happen.
  13. Let the tempeh ferment for a day.
  14. The tempeh is cut into thin slices, repeat until none is left.
  15. Mix water with vinegar, garlic, flavoring, and salt to improve the crunchiness and taste of the tempe.
  16. Prepare a wok and pour a hefty amount of oil inside it. Let it heat up until it’s hot enough to deep fry the tempeh slices.
  17. Dip the slices into the flavoring, then put them into the wok immediately. Fry until crispy.
  18. Keripik tempe is ready to be served!

Seaside Balls (Corned Beef Balls)

Our fried corned beef balls are a savory snack made from seasoned corned beef. It was shaped into small balls, then coated and deep-fried until golden and crispy. It has a crispy outer layer, with a soft and flavorful sensation inside. Because it is bite-sized, it’s easy to serve and perfect for sharing. A savory and slightly salty flavor was offered with each bite. Mixed mayonnaise and chili sauce were paired with this snack to give it extra flavor. We sell this snack under the name “Seaside Balls”, for it to also match our beach-themed booth.

In addition to its delicious taste, “Seaside Balls” is made using carefully selected ingredients to ensure quality and freshness in every batch. The corned beef mixture is prepared with the perfect balance of seasoning so that the flavor is rich, but not too powerful. After being shaped and coated, each ball is fried at the perfect temperature to achieve a crispy texture without losing its softness inside. This snack is suitable for students and families who are looking for an affordable yet satisfying treat. With its eye-catching presentation and unique beach-themed concept, “Seaside Balls” is not only tasty, but also memorable for customers who visit our booth.

Ingredients:

  • 250 gr Corned Beef
  • 1 pc sausage
  • 1 block of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 pcs egg
  • Enough bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • 4 pc garlic
  • Cooking oil
  • 1 tsp flavoring
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp tapioca flour
Instructions:
  1. Prepare the tools and ingredients.
  2. Remove the corned beef from the packaging and set it aside.
  3. Grate the garlic.
  4. Add the garlic, an egg, ground pepper, bouillon powder, soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, and salt.
  5. Stir the filling until smooth.
  6. Add the flour and tapioca flour.
  7. The filling is stirred again until evenly combined.
  8. Mix all the blended ingredients thoroughly.
  9. Prepare the mozzarella cheese (cut into cubes) as needed.
  10. Wash your hands thoroughly with running water and soap.
  11. Scoop out a spoonful of dough, roll it into a ball, flatten it, and add 1 mozzarella cheese or a slice of sausage.
  12. Roll the dough into balls for the next batch.
  13. Repeat steps 11–13 until the batter is used up.
  14. Crack the egg and stir it in a bowl.
  15. The corned beef balls are dipped into the bowl.
  16. Coat the corned beef balls in the breadcrumbs provided on the plate.
  17. Turn on the stove and add the cooking oil. Heat it.
  18. Fry the corned beef balls, turning them over so they cook evenly.
  19. Remove and drain the corned beef balls when they are golden brown.
  20. Serve on a serving plate and add the sauce.
  21. Clean the cooking area.
